EMPOWERING THE FUTURE OF SOUTH AFRICA'S IRM SECTORS

The National Business Initiative, Installation, Repair, and Maintenance (IRM) Initiative is a coordinated effort to drive inclusive economic development by strengthening local enterprises and developing critical skills, with a strong focus on the Green and Circular Economy. Our digital ecosystem connects learners, artisans, SMEs, and industry partners to the tools, knowledge, and opportunities they need to thrive.
